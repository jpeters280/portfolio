My History

I've worked in both banking and emergency services before deciding to code. I feel that my background gives me a unique perspective in life. Both fields stress self-improvement (laws and regulations change with regularity and have to be kept up on to prevent legal repercussions). Long work days have never bothered me (10 hours is a short day in emergency services) and I don't stress easily (in emergency services 'do-or-die' has a very literal meaning).

Both fields put an individual in face-to-face situations with the public. These situations helped hone my interpersonal skills and allowed me a chance to develop leadership.

For the past 5 years, I have been self-employed. While working independently the ability to stay on task is important (distractions cost money), discipline has provided me the ability to work quickly and effectively.
